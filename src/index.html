Welcome to the Solar System Simulator

Explore the 3D Solar System and discover all the planets, moons, and more!

Basic Controls

  • W / A / S / D — Move the camera
  • Mouse — Rotate the view (hold left click) / Pan the camera (hold right click)
  • Mouse wheel — Zoom in and out
  • Planets Button — On the top-left corner, select a planet to zoom in, or one of its moons
  • “Global” Button — Return to the original system view
  • “Unlock” Button — Release the camera from the selected planet
  • “Ship” Button — On the bottom-left corner, follow a spaceship orbiting the system
  • “Orbits” Button — On the top-right corner, toggle orbit visualization
  • “Help” Button — Reopen this help panel

Tips

  • You can add new moons to a planet by clicking on it
  • Take your time — each planet has detailed textures worth exploring!
